TRAVEL TIPS

Stay Hydrated
Drink plenty of water, especially during flights or in hot climates.
Learn Basic Local Phrases
Know how to say “Hello”, “Thank you”, and “Help” in the local language.
Keep Important Documents Safe
Use a travel wallet or secure pouch for passports and IDs.
Use Sunscreen
Protect your skin even on cloudy days or during winter travel.
Charge Devices Before Departure
Ensure phones and power banks are fully charged before traveling.
Download Offline Maps
Prepare for areas with limited connectivity by downloading maps in advance.
